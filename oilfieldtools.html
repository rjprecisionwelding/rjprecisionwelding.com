For the last 25 years, we have been a major producer of oil field tooling and equipment for the nation’s largest oil producing companies. We manufacture a wide variety of specialty tools, including mud screen filters. We have produced thousands of mud screens for companies in California, Florida, Louisiana, Mississippi, Oklahoma and Texas, as well as providing countless numbers of screens that are shipped globally through our bigger, 3rd party vendors.

While utilizing our close relationships with qualified vendors and our knowledge of industry requirements, we are able to provide complete, turn-key production for all down-hole drilling tools and equipment.
