R & J Precision Welding has been a family owned and operated company for over 45 years. During the mid 1970’s, Roger Doube first introduced our services to the greater Houston area as Superior Welding. As our services became known throughout the industry business boomed and many more family members joined the team – our shop eventually became known simply as: R & J Precision Welding.

Since being led by Ann and Jeremy Jordan in 1994, our quality, on time delivery and customer service has fueled us into being one of the most desired gas and oil field tooling production companies.

Throughout the years we have tried to keep our goals simple – Provide the highest quality product, keep our prices competitive within our industry and keep our current, potential and future customers 100% satisfied. We offer a complete line of exotic alloy welding services as well as a complete, turnkey production.
